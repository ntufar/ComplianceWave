# Data Sources & Research Findings

## Research Summary (Nov 2025)

### Europe
*   **Italy**: Mandatory B2B since 2019 (SDI).
*   **France**: Phased B2B mandate starting Sept 2026 (PPF/PDP).
*   **Poland**: Mandatory B2B starting Feb/April 2026 (KSeF).
*   **Germany**: Receive e-invoices from Jan 2025, Send from Jan 2027 (ZUGFeRD/XRechnung).
*   **Belgium**: Mandatory B2B from Jan 2026 (Peppol).
*   **Romania**: Mandatory B2B since Jan 2024 (RO e-Factura).
*   **Spain**: Expected B2B mandate ~2026 (VeriFactu).
*   **Denmark**: Phased B2B from Jan 2026.
*   **Greece**: Mandatory B2B since Nov 2021 (MyDATA).
*   **Croatia**: Mandatory B2B from Jan 2026.
*   **Serbia**: Mandatory B2B since Jan 2023.
*   **Slovakia**: B2G mandatory, B2B expected ~2026.
*   **Portugal**: SAF-T reporting mandatory; B2G mandatory.
*   **Hungary**: Real-time invoice reporting (RTIR) mandatory since 2018.

### Americas
*   **Brazil**: Mandatory since 2008 (NF-e).
*   **Mexico**: Mandatory since 2014 (CFDI).
*   **Chile**: Mandatory since 2018.
*   **Colombia**: Mandatory since 2020.
*   **Peru**: Mandatory since 2018.
*   **Argentina**: Mandatory since 2019.
*   **Costa Rica**: Mandatory since 2018.
*   **Uruguay**: Phased mandate completing in 2025.
*   **Paraguay**: Mandatory since 2024/2025.
*   **Ecuador**: Mandatory since 2024.
*   **USA**: No federal mandate. Market-driven (DBNAlliance).

### Asia & Pacific
*   **India**: Phased B2B since 2020 (GST e-Invoice).
*   **Saudi Arabia**: Phased B2B since 2021 (FATOORA).
*   **Malaysia**: Phased B2B starting Aug 2024 / July 2025.
*   **Singapore**: Voluntary/Soft mandate starting May 2025.
*   **Japan**: Qualified Invoice System (Peppol) since Oct 2023.
*   **Indonesia**: Mandatory since 2016.
*   **South Korea**: Mandatory since 2011.
*   **Philippines**: Phased B2B starting 2024/2025.
*   **Vietnam**: Mandatory since July 2022.
*   **China**: Fully Digitized E-Invoice pilot expanding in 2024/2025.
*   **Australia/NZ**: B2G mandates (Federal/State), B2B voluntary (Peppol).

### Africa
*   **Egypt**: Mandatory B2B since 2023.
*   **Nigeria**: Phased B2B starting 2025.
*   **Kenya**: Mandatory (eTIMS) since 2024.
*   **South Africa**: Planning phase (likely Peppol based).
*   **Zambia**: Mandatory since 2024.
